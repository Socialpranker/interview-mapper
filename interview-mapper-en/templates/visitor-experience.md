# Lens: Visitor Experience (museum/venue visitor)

**When:** interview with a visitor — to understand the visit experience from entry to exit, emotions, barriers, value.
**Unit:** code. Cell = **quote → What it reveals → → Recommendation**. No topic — "Not mentioned".
**Collection discipline:** facts about the past visit, not hypotheticals ("what would you want"). See The Mom Test.

## Layer 1 — operational (fact + quote + line)
- **V1 Visit context** [open-ended] — who, with whom, occasion, first/repeat visit.
- **V2 Path to the visit** [open-ended] — how they heard about the museum, expectations before entry, barriers at entry (ticket, wayfinding, timing).
- **V3 Delight points** [list+quote] — what hooked them, emotional peaks; specific halls/exhibits/moments.
- **V4 Friction points** [list] — wayfinding, logistics, queues, payment (QR/cashier), restrooms, seating, noise, crowds.
- **V5 Understanding the content** [open-ended] — what they did/didn't understand; the role of the tour, labels, media; where they got lost.
- **V6 Social layer** [open-ended] — who they came with, kids, photos, discussion, "Instagrammability".
- **V7 Unmet demand** [list+quote] — what they looked for and didn't find (café, shop, online ticket, program, souvenir).
- **V8 Value and intent** [open-ended] — why they came, what they take away, whether they'll return, what they'll tell friends.

## Layer 2 — analytical (conclusion + quote; CAPS keyword) — to council
- **A1 Visitor eNPS** [category] — PROMOTER / NEUTRAL / DETRACTOR + what would shift them toward promoter. *(unstable)*
- **A2 Emotional arc** — where the peak is, where the dip is over the course of the visit (entry → exhibition → exit).
- **A3 Moment of truth** — the point that determined the overall impression (one detail outweighed the rest).
- **A4 Key barrier** — structural (building, location — can't be changed) vs operational (payment, wayfinding — solvable).
- **A5 Visit metaphor** — how they figuratively describe the experience; what it says about their perception.
- **A6 Key insight** — 1 sentence: visitor [segment] came for [value], but [barrier/peak], therefore [implication for the museum].

## Forcing questions
When were you last there — what did you do, step by step? What surprised you (good/bad)? Where did you get confused? What did you look for and not find? Who did you tell what afterward? Will you return — what for?
