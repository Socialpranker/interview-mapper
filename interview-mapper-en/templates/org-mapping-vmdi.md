# Lens: Employee Org-Mapping — VMDI codebook (6 blocks · 40 codes)

**When:** interview with a VMDI employee following the employee guide. This is an example codebook (museum employees) — a REAL project codebook (not a reconstruction).
**Unit:** code. Each code is filled in as **quote → What it reveals → → Recommendation**.
**Output format per code:**
```
**C3.2 | Incident vs pattern** [category: Incident / Pattern / Not mentioned]
Category: PATTERN
What it reveals: [interpretation — what this code reveals about the system]
_"verbatim quote" (Lxx)_
→ Recommendation: [concrete action; if none — "—"]
```
Code types: **Open-ended text** (quote+interpretation), **Category** (closed choice + quote), **List** (a set from a reference list + quote). If the topic didn't come up — "Not mentioned".
**Δ (1–5)** — when comparing AI↔Human: 1 full divergence · 3 partial · 5 full match. Calculated per block (see `references/rubric.md`).
**System-priority rule (Block 3):** the same episode recurring across **≥3 employees from different departments = system priority** (in synthesis this is triangulation `score_insights --k 3` by role).

---

## BLOCK 1. Professional path and dynamics of the relationship with the museum
_Goal: context of presence, deliberateness of the choice, internal eNPS._
- **C1.1 Circumstances of arrival** [open-ended] — when/where they came from, what brought them to this museum specifically; a deliberate choice or chance.
- **C1.2 Alternatives at the time of hiring** [open-ended] — did they consider other places; why VMDI (subject matter/people/stability/other).
- **C1.3 Expectation/reality gap** [open-ended] — what turned out unexpected in the first months (+/−).
- **C1.4 Motivators and demotivators** [list+quote] — Motivators: Mission/Colleagues/Professional growth/Stability/Other · Demotivators: Bureaucracy/Salary/Management/Other.
- **C1.5 Internal eNPS** [category+quote] — Promoter / Neutral / Detractor / Not mentioned. *(unstable cell → council S3)*

## BLOCK 2. Experience of effective professional work
_Goal: the system at its best, reproducibility, bottom-up initiative, cross-department interaction._
- **C2.1 Effective case** [open-ended] — the project/task of the year they're proud of; the stages.
- **C2.2 Source of the initiative** [category] — Bottom-up / Top-down / Situational / Not mentioned.
- **C2.3 Cross-department interaction** [open-ended] — who was involved, how communication was built.
- **C2.4 Speed of decisions and approvals** [open-ended] — levels of approval; delays even in the best case = a system signal.
- **C2.5 Hidden risks / fragility** [open-ended] — what could have gone wrong but didn't.
- **C2.6 Culture of recognition** [category+quote] — Acknowledged by management / By colleagues / By visitors / Not acknowledged / Not mentioned. *(unstable → council)*
- **C2.7 Reproducibility of the result** [category] — Systemic process / Personal enthusiasm / Mixed / Not mentioned.
- **C2.P Role-specific question** [open-ended] — depends on the employee's function; verbatim quote.

## BLOCK 3. Experience of operational failures and systemic constraints
_Goal: a map of systemic failures. Each episode is a point where a process breaks._
- **C3.1 Failure episode** [open-ended] — a situation with disproportionate costs.
- **C3.2 Incident vs pattern** [category] — Incident / Pattern / Not mentioned.
- **C3.3 Cause attribution** [list] — Competence / Process / Resources / Communication / Mixed / Not mentioned.
- **C3.4 Zone of responsibility** [open-ended] — who could fix it but doesn't; the "nobody's problem" issue.
- **C3.5 Attempts to fix** [open-ended] — were any made, the result, where the "wall" is.
- **C3.6 Loss estimate** [open-ended] — time/financial losses.
- **C3.7 Opportunity cost** [open-ended] — what they'd do with the freed-up resources.
- **C3.P Role-specific question** [open-ended] — positioning/reality gap by function.

## BLOCK 4. Internal communications and cascading of strategy
_Goal: how strategy reaches employees, information gaps, silos, bottom-up initiative._
- **C4.1 Channels for receiving information** [open-ended] — formal and informal.
- **C4.2 Transparency of management** [open-ended] — did they learn of a decision after the fact.
- **C4.3 Strategy penetration** [category+quote] — Articulates / Partially / Doesn't articulate / Not mentioned.
- **C4.4 Cross-department silos** [list] — with whom it works effectively, with whom it's difficult; specific pairs.
- **C4.5 Throughput of bottom-up initiative** [category+quote] — Present / Formally present, doesn't work / Absent / Not mentioned.
- **C4.6 Employee agency** [open-ended] — specific episodes of influencing decisions.

## BLOCK 5. Visitor experience through the lens of professional observation
_Goal: employees' observations about visitors — an underrated source; patterns outside surveys._
- **C5.1 Memorable episode** [open-ended] — the most recent interaction with a visitor.
- **C5.2 Pattern vs exception** [category] — Pattern / Exception / Not mentioned.
- **C5.3 Recurring requests / difficulties** [list] — friction points along the visitor's path.
- **C5.4 Audience segmentation** [open-ended] — differences in the audience across areas.
- **C5.5 Retention / regular visitors** [open-ended] — reason for returning; is it tracked.
- **C5.6 Typical visitor + barriers** [open-ended] — who comes; what keeps the rest away (structural vs operational barriers).
- **C5.7 Priority change in the experience** [open-ended] — one aspect from entry to exit.

## BLOCK 6. Priorities and direction of development
_Goal: priorities and anti-priorities from the inside, intangible needs, forecast._
- **C6.1 Metaphor for the mission** [open-ended] — an image of the museum in non-official language.
- **C6.2 Anchors — what to preserve** [list+quote] — what must not be touched even during reforms.
- **C6.3 Anti-priorities** [list+quote] — which practices to abandon (often more valuable than the list of priorities).
- **C6.4 Intangible needs** [list] — Authority / Information / Autonomy / Recognition / Other.
- **C6.5 Advice to a new director** [open-ended] — the most honest answer of the interview.
- **C6.6 Forecast in 3 years** [category+quote] — Optimism / Anxiety / Apathy / Not mentioned. *(unstable → council)*
