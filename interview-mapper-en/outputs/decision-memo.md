# Output: Decision memo (memo for a decision)

**When:** the goal is to distill interviews into a short document supporting a specific stakeholder decision.
**Input:** insights + (if available) human↔AI comparison. Shorter than insight cards, tailored to 1 decision.

## Format (1–2 pages)
```
# Decision: [the question we're answering]
## Recommendation
[one clear action/choice + for whom]
## What it's based on (3–5 insights)
- [insight] — X of N interviews, criticality — «quote» (Interview, Lxx)
## Risks and counterarguments
- [what argues against it; who would disagree; where data is thin]
## What we DON'T know
- [open questions, gaps, silence in the data]
## Confidence: high/medium/low
[based on triangulation + grounding + n interviews]
```

## Rules
- One specific recommendation. If there's no data to support it, say so — don't stretch.
- The counterarguments and "what we don't know" sections are mandatory — otherwise it's a presentation, not a decision.
- Every support point is a verified quote + interview count. Latent conclusions (eNPS, etc.) are marked as requiring a human.
- n<k → "pilot, not a basis for an irreversible decision".
